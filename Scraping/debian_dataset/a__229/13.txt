This behaviour is configurable.  For armel and armhf we enable
CONFIG_ARM_ATAG_DTB_COMPAT and
CONFIG_ARM_ATAG_DTB_COMPAT_CMDLINE_FROM_BOOTLOADER so that ATAG
variables override the DTB.
