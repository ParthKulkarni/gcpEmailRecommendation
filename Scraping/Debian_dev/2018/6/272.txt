IAPWS-IF97 is the Industrial Formulation by The International
Association for the Properties of Water and Steam. This library implements
the IF97 formulation for the properties of pure water in fast, optimized
standard C++ code in a single header.
This library is a dependency for CoolProp, and I plan on packaging both
under the Science Team.
