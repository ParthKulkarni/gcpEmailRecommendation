This is the Osmocom (Open Source Mobile Communications) implementation of an
analog cellular mobile phone systems, among them amps, a-netz, b-netz, c-netz,
nmt, (j)tacs, radiocom2000. This package also contains binaries for c-netz
sim card simulation and osmocom analog TV signal instrumentation. It will be
maintained by debian-mobcom group. The package doesn't need a sponsor.
