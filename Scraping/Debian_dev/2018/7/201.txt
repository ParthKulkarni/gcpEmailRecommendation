  Description     : Perl 6 package manager
Zef is a Perl 6 package (module) manager. It can be used to download
and install Perl 6 modules in your home directory or as a system-wide 
module.
zef is useful as a central module of the Perl 6 ecosystem. Once more
Perl 6 modules have been packaged, many people will use these packages
instead of direct downloads via zef, but at least for development zef
will remain important.
There is a Perl 6 / rakudo packaging group and we plan to maintain all
Perl 6 modules together.
