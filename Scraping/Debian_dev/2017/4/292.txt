  Description     : Python module to generate QR Codes
The pyqrcode module is a QR code generator that is simple to use and
written in pure Python. The module can automate most of the building
process for creating QR codes. Most codes can be created using only
two lines of code.
Unlike other generators, all of the helpers can be controlled manually.
You are free to set any or all of the properties of your QR code. QR
codes can be saved as SVG, PNG, and plain text. They can also be
displayed directly in most Linux terminal emulators.
The pyqrcode module attempts to follow the QR code standard as closely
as possible. The terminology, encodings and algorithms used in pyqrcode
come directly from the standard.
